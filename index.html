Anas Barakat

Postdoctoral researcher

ETH Zurich

I am an ETH-FDS postdoctoral fellow at ETH Zurich working with Niao He within the Optimization & Decision Intelligence Group in the department of computer science. My research interests include reinforcement learning and stochastic optimization for machine learning.

I obtained my PhD in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Institut Polytechnique de Paris at Télécom Paris in the S2A (Signal, Statistics and Learning) team under the supervision of Pascal Bianchi and Walid Hachem. I received my engineering Master’s degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science from Télécom Paris and a Master’s degree in Data Science from Université Paris Saclay.

You can find more information about me on my CV.
